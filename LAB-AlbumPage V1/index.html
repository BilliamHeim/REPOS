Recess

Skrillex

Released March 18th, 2014

Skrillex's first and currently only studio release album, Recess was recorded between 2013 and 2014 while he was on a world tour. Although it received mixed reviews from critics, Recess charted in multiple countries and was his highest charted release here in the United States and the UK, debuting at number 4 on the US Billboard 200 chart. Rolling Stone later also ranked it at 22 on their "50 Best Albums of 2014" list.
